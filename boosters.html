About the Band Boosters

The mission of the Turner Band Booster Club is to continually support the students, directors, and families of the Turner High School Band in their goal to achieve the highest standards of music education and music performance; facilitate the musical enrichment of the student membership of Turner High and its feeder schools; promote the place and value of music in our community and the significant role music plays in the cultural, intellectual, and social lives of our children; and foster fellowship and goodwill among its members and provide its membership with opportunities for personal growth and leadership.

Below are some fun and easy ways to get involved in the Turner Band.

The students need and appreciate your support of their hard work.

 

  • Chaperones for games, competitions, trips, lock-ins
  • Pit Crew to help with percussion instruments, props, sound equipment, loading and unloading instruments/equipment are on wheels
  • Concessions for Tuesday Middle School Games and Friday night home games. This is one of the biggest money-makers for the band!
  • Water Distributor at games and contests
  • Bleacher Clean-up crew after games
  • Prop design and construction
  • Printing
  • Meal servers for the band before home games
  • Meal cooking for directors on long days
  • Summer band fruit cutters for band breaks
  • Assisting at feeder school band instrument drives
  • Truck driver for away games and competitions
  • Luggage monitor to keep an eye on all luggage before loading for a trip
  • Merch Booth at events (band spirit wear, pit crew salsa, scrip gift cards) Fund Raising goods distributor (cookie dough)
